💡 Standard Format Guidelines

We support university-standard attendance register templates. Columns should contain REGD.NO, NAME, followed by subject codes (e.g. MEFA, LCS), with conducted hours in row 2, and student cells formatted as attended(conducted) (e.g. 12(18)).
